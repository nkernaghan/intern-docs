About Haven256

Haven256 is a crypto forensics and digital asset intelligence firm. We build tools that trace illicit funds, detect scam wallets, and support law enforcement and compliance teams. This summer, we're launching new internal products — and we need financial expertise to support their strategy, pricing, and go-to-market planning.

The Role

You'll own the financial strategy and analysis for one of two strategic projects, working closely with the engineering and leadership teams. This isn't administrative work — you'll be building financial models, analyzing market dynamics, and influencing product and pricing decisions for tools that could become commercial products or internal operational systems.
